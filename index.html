South Penrith Veterinary Clinic has cared for animals for over 35 years. Our team offers high quality healthy checks and treatments for a variety of animals. We have a special interest in reptiles, wild life, pocket pets and chickens in addition to our passion towards cats and dogs. Whether it be a surgery in a snake or a behavioural consult for a mischievous dog, we have confidence in offering the best option for each individual patient.
